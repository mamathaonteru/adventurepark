Important things to keep in mind when visiting Wonderla Amusement Park

1.Carry valid ID proofs, face masks, sanitiser, sunglasses, sunscreen lotion, extra pair of clothes, and flip-flops

2.Download and register on Aarogya Setu app

3.Buy tickets in advance to avoid long queues and maintain social distancing at the ticket counter

4.Do not dive in the pools, as they are shallow

5.People with COVID-19 symptoms, high blood pressure and heart conditions, pregnant women, and those prone to epilepsy should refrain from visiting the park

6.You must follow all safety and hygiene instructions

7.Do not carry outside foods and drinks inside the park

8.Take care of your phone, wallet and other belongings

9.1Avoid wearing loose garments for safety reasons

10.Stay hydrated, especially on warmer days
